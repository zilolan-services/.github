# About

**Zilolan Services** is the engineering organization behind **AI Everywhere**, focused on delivering modern software solutions powered by artificial intelligence.

We build scalable systems for startups, enterprises, universities, and public organizations by combining cloud-native technologies with Large Language Models (LLMs).

Our engineering team covers the entire software lifecycle—from architecture design and product development to AI deployment, cloud infrastructure, and long-term maintenance.
